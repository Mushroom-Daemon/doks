The goal of the Kickass Copy Playbook is to help you write kickass copy that converts.

Achieving this goal requires no special talent or creative genius on your end. You simply have to be willing to conduct a bit of research and put in some practice. Do that, and you'll be writing dynamic, persuasive letters, ads, et. al. in no time.

To get started, go through the Research Questionaires. These checklists can be thought through on your own, with your client, or with their customers.

Once you know who you're writing to---and what you're selling---the Kickass Copy Essentials chrecklists and tips in All Write, Already! will help you pump out copy that creates strong brand associations and persuades your reader to action.
