The best testimonials tell a story.

Discover your client’s story—and how you fit into it—with the following prompts (adjusted as needed).
* What problem were you trying to solve before working with us? What was your vision?
* Why did you choose [insert company] over your other options?
* Describe your experience during the project.
    * Was it in line with your expectations?
    * What reservations or fears did you have?
    * What most pleasantly surprised you about working with us?
    * What would you say to someone on the fence about hiring us?
* What results have you seen in your [daily/professional] life since work was completed?
* If you could magically improve one thing about your experience with us, what would it be? (Be honest. It helps us serve you better!)
