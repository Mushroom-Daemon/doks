 ### How to Brainstorm Taglines

 1. Emotional Outpouring: Write everything you know/feel about the brand
 2. Cut down to 10-15 words
 3. Play around with them!

 Rules
 * Come up with a three word tagline
 * Maybe four...
 * sometimes five...
