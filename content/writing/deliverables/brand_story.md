### One Liner
Many homeowners want a fresh exterior for their home but get worried about choosing a contractor. With Smucker Exteriors you get a beautiful home that’s dry, safe and energy efficient—along with a contracting experience to remember.

### Tagline Ideas

### A character

Homeowner who wants to update their home’s exterior. This may be for functional reasons (the roof has a leak, siding is cracked, etc.) but most often is because clients don’t like the look of their home.
### Has a problem
The problem a typical Smucker Exterior customer faces is being unsure of who to trust as a contractor. This makes them nervous—they don’t want to be taken advantage of. Smucker Exteriors is here to free them from that worry and help them enjoy the remodeling process.
### And meets a guide
Smucker Exteriors knows how important your home is—and our guys have been keeping customers’ homes energy efficient, safe and dry for over 6 years. And we’re just getting started.
